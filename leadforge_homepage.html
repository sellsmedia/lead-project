FAQ
Common questions
What exactly is a lead? ↓
Each lead is a real local business with a verified Google Business Profile but no linked website — meaning they have digital intent and a visible need for your services.
How fresh is the data? ↓
Every lead is re-verified every 90 days. If a business adds a website, we remove it from inventory and auto-refund any unlock within 14 days. You always see the last verified date.
What if the contact info is wrong? ↓
Click "Report bad lead." If both the phone and email fail our automated check, you get 1 credit back within 60 seconds. No forms, no waiting, no arguments.
Do credits expire? ↓
Credits last 12 months from purchase, and they reset if you buy any new pack within that window — so active users effectively never lose credits.
Can I share leads with my team? ↓
Agency and Scale plans include team credit sharing. Multiple users can share one wallet and see each other's unlocked leads inside the dashboard.
Is there an API? ↓
Yes — Scale plan includes metered API access. Great for agencies or website builders who want to programmatically query and unlock leads at volume.
